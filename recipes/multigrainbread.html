Ingredients:

  • water
  • yeast
  • whole meal flour
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • sunflower seeds
  • pumpkin seeds
  • chia seeds

Let's get into this!

  1. Combine warm water (like bath water, or 110 degrees F / 43 C), yeast, maple syrup or sugar, salt, flax seed and flours in a large mixing bowl and stir. The result will be a sticky, rough dough.

  2. Knead and turn it in the bowl with your hands. Add flour until the dough is no longer sticking to the sides

  3. Cover and let rise for 2 hours at room temperature

  4. Use fingers to create a small hole in the dough and pour in sunflower seeds and oats. Transfer dough to a lightly floured work surface and knead about 20 turns or until elastic. Form into a loaf-like shape.

  5. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (218 C). Bake the bread for 26 to 35 minutes, or until deep golden brown and risen.
